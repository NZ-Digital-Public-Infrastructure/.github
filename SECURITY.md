# Security Policy

The Government Digital Delivery Agency (GDDA) welcomes reports of security vulnerabilities in these repositories and the services they document.

## Reporting a vulnerability
**Preferred**: use GitHub's private vulnerability reporting ("Report a vulnerability" on the Security tab of this repo).
Or email: [GovernmentApp@gdda.govt.nz](mailto:GovernmentApp@gdda.govt.nz)

For vulnerabilities in NZ government systems generally, you may also report to the NZ National Cyber Security Centre (NCSC) under its Coordinated Vulnerability Disclosure policy: [https://www.ncsc.govt.nz/report/](https://www.ncsc.govt.nz/report/)

Please do not open public issues for security reports.

We aim to acknowledge reports within 2 business days and will keep you updated through to resolution. We support coordinated disclosure and will credit reporters who wish to be acknowledged.
